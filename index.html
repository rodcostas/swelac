About SWELAC

A Forum for Bilateral Economic Cooperation

Trade and investment between regions often depend on networks, institutional trust, and access to decision-makers. SWELAC provides a structured forum for companies, institutions, and leaders to strengthen these connections across the Atlantic.

🤝
Institutional Collaboration
Facilitating dialogue between Swedish and Latin American public and private institutions to align trade and investment frameworks.
📊
Trade Flow Intelligence
Providing members with economic data, market analysis, and sector insights to support informed investment decisions.
🌐
Regional Partnerships
Building long-term economic partnerships across Sweden and the full Latin American region, from Mexico to Patagonia.
📅
Future Opportunities
Identifying emerging sectors and bilateral investment opportunities, including green transition, innovation, and infrastructure.
