Inauguration 2025
SWELAC Formally Inaugurated at the Swedish Parliament
The formal inauguration of SWELAC took place at the Swedish Parliament, joined by ambassadors from across Latin America, members of the Riksdag, representatives from Swedish industry, and SWELAC board members. Co-hosted with GRULAC, with remarks from Pro Tempore Chair Ambassador Patricia Godínez of El Salvador on innovation, entrepreneurship, and the growing bilateral relationship. Chairman Nils Yacoob Ekman presented recent commercial developments. A Letter of Intent was signed with the Embassy of Ecuador, establishing a framework expandable to other countries across the region.
🏛️ Swedish Parliament 🤝 GRULAC 📄 LOI · Embassy of Ecuador 🇸🇪 Stockholm

Trade Dialogue 2025
Sweden–El Salvador Trade Round Table in San Salvador
SWELAC convened a structured trade dialogue in San Salvador addressing digital infrastructure, high-technology exports, and market entry frameworks. Participants included the Embassy of Sweden, Ministerio de Economía, Business Sweden, CEPA, Bitrefill, K1 Technology, ASAFINTECH, and XFounders Accelerator. Represented by President Nils Yacoob Ekman and Representative Rodrigo Gómez.

Agreement 2025
Letter of Intent Signed with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of El Salvador
SWELAC and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of El Salvador signed a bilateral collaboration framework — Vice Minister Adriana Mira and President Nils Yacoob Ekman. Three pillars: Trade & Investment, Innovation & Digitalization, and Sustainability & Green Transition.

Event 2025
SWELAC Hosts Reception Honoring Humanitarian Medical Delegation
SWELAC hosted a reception in San Salvador honoring Spanish and Swedish ophthalmologists conducting a visual health mission. Attended by Vice Minister Patricia Godínez, Ambassador of Spain Sonia Alvarez Cibanal, Swedish Ambassador Pontus Rosenberg, and members of the diplomatic corps.

Who We Are

About SWELAC

SWELAC is a bilateral chamber of commerce facilitating trade, investment, and institutional cooperation between Swedish and Latin American ecosystems.

🤝
Institutional Collaboration
Facilitating dialogue between Swedish and Latin American public and private institutions to align trade and investment frameworks.
📊
Trade Flow Intelligence
Providing members with economic data, market analysis, and sector insights to support informed investment decisions.
🌱
Sustainability & Green Transition
Advancing circular economy initiatives, renewable energy, and resilient supply chains across both regions.
🌐
Regional Partnerships
Building long-term economic partnerships across Sweden and the full Latin American region, from Mexico to Patagonia.
